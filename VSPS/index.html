ABOUT HARMONY,DR VSPS CANTT
AN OVERVIEW
    Dr. Virendra Swarup Public School Cantt celebrates Harmony once a year, it has the capacity to inspire us and give us lots of moments to enjoy. Harmony also plays a huge role in leadership skills and overall personality development as it’s the biggest event organized by our school. It gives us all a break from daily chores and provides unlimited fun. It has many events which curtail to a lot of talents and after the competitive thrill is over we end the event with the musical night which has special guest appearances.
    Dr. Virendra Swarup Public School Cantt is yet again all set to celebrate its annual techno-cultural, arts and literary festival "Harmony". "Harmony" provides an opportunity to all the students to display their talents in various fields. The key objectives of such events is to unravel the hidden skills in each youngsters thereby boosting their self-confidence and self belief.
AWARD AND RECOGNITION
    There are to be won 8 category championships and over all trophies will be awarded on participation in the maximum number of events in each category.
    Teams/Participants will be Point System: awarded points as follows First Position: 5 points Second Position: 3 points Third Position: 1 point.
    The team scoring the maximum number of points shall win the ‘Best Team Trophy'.In addition to the Best Team Trophy, individual trophies and certiﬁcates will be awarded to all winners runners up and in individual events will be awarded to all Participation certiﬁcates members of the participating teams.
